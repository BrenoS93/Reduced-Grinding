Reduced Grinding

====INFO====

Open Source at GitHub. Report issues there or at home page (Note Steam Workshop page doesn't notify me of comments).

This mod will prevent having to spend huge amounts of time farming loot and adds other conveniences. It's highly configurable. Here's a summary of the features (see forum page for more info).

- Increased item chances for certain enemy drops. Item values scale to new drop rates by default.
- Potion for increasing fishing bobber amount.
- Angler Quest resets when all Angler Armor wearing players have finished their quest.
- Fishing Tokens when finishing Quests, to purchase items from the Fish Merchant.
- Moonball to advance Moon Phase or skip the Blood Moon.
- Greater + Super Battle Potion to increase enemy spawns.
- Holiday timeline for periodic Halloween and Christmas days.
- Cancel Invasions when all players go underground.
- Helpful recipes including Universal Plyon; Universal Pylon recipe has 3 difficulty settings.

====OFF BY DEFAULT====

- Reduce Bestiary requirement for Universal Pylon.
- Staff of Difficulty. Changes difficulty settings.
- Faster time flow while sleeping.
