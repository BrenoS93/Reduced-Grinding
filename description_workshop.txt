Reduced Grinding (Info below is for the 1.4.4+ version of tModLoader)

====INFO====

Open Source at GitHub. Report issues there or at home page.

This mod will prevent having to spend huge amounts of time farming loot and adds other conveniences. Features can be configured or disabled. Here's a summary (it's also at the tModLoader Wiki):

- Increased item chances for a few enemy loot drops.
- Item values scale to new drop rates by default.
- Angler Quest per day gradually increases from game progression. Has a short cooldown
    if another unfinished player has the current quest fish.
- Greater + Super Battle Potion to increase enemy spawns.
- Items that activate Christmas and Halloween for the current day. By default, crafting
    requires their matching season.
- Item for canceling invasions.
- Recipe for Universal Pylon. Disable or Customize.
- 1/75 chance for chest to generate with Terragrim. Chance can be customized or
    disabled.

====OFF BY DEFAULT====

- Change Universal Pylon Bestiary requirement.
- Configuraitons for changing enemy loot drop chances.
- Adjust Bed + Enchanted Dial time rate and cooldown.
- Potions for increasing fishing bobber amount.
- Fishing Coins when finishing Quests, to purchase Quest Items from the Pirate NPC.
- Staff of Difficulty. Changes world difficulty mode.
- Moon Watch to advance Moon Phase.
- Witch Doctor sells Chlorophyte after Plantera.
